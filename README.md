# Sanjeevani – Integrated Emergency Health Infrastructure

### Team Name: 4-Bits

---

## Overview

Sanjeevani is a unified digital health ecosystem designed to address India's fragmented emergency and rural healthcare system.

The platform enables **instant access to critical medical information during emergencies**, improves coordination between **patients, hospitals, ambulances, and caregivers**, and supports **rural healthcare accessibility** through offline-first technology.

Our mission is simple:

> **In emergencies, lack of information costs lives. Sanjeevani ensures the right data reaches the right hands at the right time.**

---

# Problem Statement

## India's Emergency & Rural Healthcare Crisis

India's healthcare system suffers from **fragmentation, delayed emergency response, and lack of continuity of care**, particularly in rural and time-critical situations.

### Current Challenges

During emergencies:

- Responders often **lack access to patient blood group, allergies, chronic conditions, and medications**
- Medical records are **scattered across hospitals and paper prescriptions**
- Caregivers **cannot access patient health data in real time**

### Consequences

- Delayed treatment during the **Golden Hour**
- Increased **medical errors**
- **Preventable deaths**

---

# India-Specific Gaps

- **1.6+ lakh road accident deaths annually**
- No **unified emergency medical data system**
- **Severe rural doctor shortages**
- **75%+ rural patients unaware of telemedicine**
- **Poor internet connectivity in low-resource areas**

---

# Core Problem

India currently lacks:

> **A secure, integrated, real-time emergency health infrastructure that connects patients, ambulances, hospitals, and caregivers across both urban and rural regions.**

---

# Our Solution – Sanjeevani

Sanjeevani creates a **single unified digital health infrastructure** to enable faster emergency response and improved healthcare coordination.

---

# Key Features

## 1. Secure Digital Health Records

- ABHA / ABDM compatible system
- Centralized patient health profile
- Structured emergency health data
- Consent-based data sharing
- Role-based access control
- Secure audit logs
- Offline emergency data caching

---

## 2. Real-Time Emergency Intelligence

- Unique **Emergency QR Code for each patient**
- Instant **limited emergency data view**
- Time-bound access logs
- **One-tap SOS activation**
- Geo-location based hospital suggestions
- Ambulance call integration
- Caregiver alert notifications
- Voice-guided CPR and emergency assistance

---

## 3. AI-Powered Assistance (Non-Diagnostic)

- Medication explanation in simple language
- Dosage clarification
- Side-effect awareness
- Multilingual prescription interpretation
- Advisory symptom triage

---

## 4. Rural Accessibility Layer

Designed specifically for low-resource environments.

- Offline-first architecture
- ASHA / Community Health Worker mode
- Assisted ABHA enrollment
- Mobile clinic integration
- Low-bandwidth fallback (SMS / USSD)

---

### National Infrastructure Already Exists

- **73+ crore ABHA IDs issued**
- **Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) operational**

- **Telemedicine Practice Guidelines (2020)**

### Proven Digital Adoption

- e-Sanjeevani delivered **10M+ teleconsultations**
- State trauma IT systems already improving care coordination
